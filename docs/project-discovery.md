# GreenSpring Website Project Discovery

## Brand Assets
- **Logo**: Provided - circular green/blue gradient design with "GreenSpring" text
- **Tagline**: "Management & Marketing"
- **Color Scheme**: Black, Blue, Green with circle themes

## Business Focus Questions

### 1. Target Market & Services
- What types of businesses does he typically transform? (Size, industry, stage)
- What are his core service offerings? (Strategy, operations, leadership, turnarounds, etc.)
- Does he have specific methodologies or frameworks he's known for?
- What's his unique value proposition vs other consultants?

### 2. Experience & Credibility
- What credentials/achievements should be highlighted? (Years experience, certifications, notable clients)
- Does he have client testimonials or case studies to feature?
- Any speaking engagements, publications, or media mentions?
- What's his preferred professional tone? (Authoritative, approachable, innovative, etc.)

### 3. Client Interaction & Functionality
- How does he prefer initial client contact? (Phone, email, contact form, calendar booking)
- Will he want to publish content regularly? (Blog, insights, market updates)
- Any specific calls-to-action? (Free consultation, assessment, download resources)
- Does he offer different service tiers or packages?

### 4. Design & Brand Preferences
- Any existing brand assets beyond the logo? (Business cards, letterhead, presentations)
- What does "sleek" mean to him? (Minimalist, modern, sophisticated, tech-forward)
- How should the circle theme be incorporated? (Subtle accents, major design element, icons)
- Preferred imagery style? (Corporate, lifestyle, abstract, photography vs illustrations)

## Proposed Site Structure

### Core Pages
1. **Home** - Hero section, value proposition, key services overview
2. **About** - Personal story, credentials, philosophy, professional headshot
3. **Services** - Detailed breakdown of consulting offerings
4. **Case Studies/Results** - Success stories and transformations
5. **Contact** - Contact form, phone, email, consultation booking

### Additional Pages (Optional)
6. **Resources/Insights** - Blog, whitepapers, thought leadership content
7. **Process** - How he works with clients (methodology)
8. **Industries** - Specific sectors he specializes in (if applicable)

## Technical Notes
- **Hosting**: Static site (Netlify/Vercel recommended)
- **Technology**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Domain**: Custom domain needed
- **Features**: Responsive design, contact forms, potential blog functionality
